Simple Garden Salad with Vinaigrette

Appropriately sized description

Crisp lettuce, cucumbers, and cherry tomatoes get tossed with a tangy Dijon vinaigrette. Ready in minutes, it pairs with almost any main course and keeps well for a light lunch the next day.

Ingrediants:

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, torn into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ English cucumber, sliced
  • ¼ small red onion, thinly sliced
  • ⅓ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 small clove garlic, minced (optional)
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Wash and dry the lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, and onion.
  3. Combine lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, and onion in a large salad bowl.
  4. In a small jar with a tight lid, add olive oil, vinegar, Dijon, garlic (if using), salt, and pepper. Seal and shake until emulsified.
  5. Taste the dressing and adjust salt, pepper, or vinegar as needed.
  6. Drizzle dressing over the salad and toss gently until evenly coated.
  7. Serve immediately.
  8. Enjoy!
