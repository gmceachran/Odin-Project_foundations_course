Best Big, Fat, Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

Appropriately sized description

These cookies are crispy at the edges, soft in the middle, and packed with chocolate chips. The dough rests briefly so the flour hydrates—no chilling overnight required for bakery-style chew and spread.

Ingrediants:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Line cookie sheets with parchment paper.
  3. Sift together the flour, baking soda, and salt; set aside.
  4. In a medium bowl, cream together the melted butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until well blended.
  5. Beat in the vanilla, egg, and egg yolk until light and creamy.
  6. Mix in the sifted ingredients until just blended.
  7. Stir in the chocolate chips by hand using a wooden spoon.
  8. Drop cookie dough by rounded tablespoons onto the prepared pans, placing cookies several inches apart.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are lightly toasted, about 15 minutes.
  10. Cool on baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
  11. Enjoy!
